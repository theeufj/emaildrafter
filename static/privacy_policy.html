Aidrafter.xyz Privacy Policy

Introduction

Aidrafter Pty Ltd ("Aidrafter," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our website, Aidrafter.xyz.

Personal Data We Collect

  • Information you provide to us: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when you create an account, contact us, or submit information through the website.
  • Information we collect automatically: This includes technical information about your device, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times. We also collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and links you click.

How We Use Your Personal Data

  • To provide you with access to our website and services, such as generating responses to your emails.
  • To improve our website and services, such as by analyzing how you use our website and identifying areas for improvement.
  • To communicate with you about our website, services, and other matters.

Data Handling and Storage

We do not store any customer emails. When you use our service, your emails are passed directly to Google's Gemini large language model for processing. We do not retain, store, or archive any email content on our servers. This approach ensures maximum privacy and security for your sensitive information.

How We Use Your Data Provided By Google

  • To read your emails in your Google inbox to provide context for responses generated by AiDrafter. These emails are not stored by us and are processed in real-time.
  • To compose emails similar to your previous responses, using Google's Gemini LLM.
  • To determine your identity by verifying your email address and full name.

How We Use Your Data Provided By Microsoft

  • To read your emails in your Microsoft inbox to provide context for responses generated by AiDrafter. As with Google emails, these are not stored by us and are processed in real-time.
  • To compose emails similar to your previous responses, using Google's Gemini LLM.
  • To verify your identity using data from Microsoft, such as your email address and full name.

Generative Model Usage

AiDrafter utilizes Google's Gemini large language model to generate tailored responses to your emails. We do not use any other models and are bound by Google's Data and Privacy Policy. Your emails are passed directly to this model for processing and are not stored by us. You can find more details about Google's data handling practices here.

Use of Google Workspace APIs

We explicitly affirm that we do not use Google Workspace APIs to develop, improve, or train generalized AI and/or ML models. The data obtained through Google Workspace APIs is used solely for the purpose of providing our specific service to you, as described in this privacy policy. We do not retain or use this data for any other purposes, including the development or enhancement of generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models. Your emails are passed directly to Google's Gemini LLM and are not stored on our servers.

Legal Basis for Processing Your Personal Data

  • Consent: You have given us consent to process your data for specific purposes.
  • Legitimate interests: We process your data to provide services, improve our website, and communicate with you.
  • Compliance with law: We may process your data to comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help operate our website and services (e.g., hosting, payment processing, analytics).
  • Other third parties: If required by law or to protect rights, investigate fraud, or ensure the safety of users.

Your Data Protection Rights

  • The right to access your data.
  • The right to request corrections of inaccuracies.
  • The right to request erasure, subject to legal exceptions.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless otherwise required by law. As stated earlier, we do not store any of your emails - they are processed in real-time and are not retained on our servers.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our no-storage policy for emails adds an extra layer of security to your sensitive information.
